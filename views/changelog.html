Changelog - [2.1.7]

21 September 2024

New Commands:

/blocked-roles toggle: Enables you to toggle specific roles to block them from earning entries in the chat rewards system. Add or remove a role from the list, ensuring that users with blocked roles will no longer earn entries.

/blocked-roles list: Lists all roles that are currently blocked from earning entries. Displays a list of roles that are prohibited from earning entries.

Other Changes:

Bug fixes.

Changelog - [2.1.6]

17 September 2024

Improved webhook validation to automatically handle and recreate missing webhooks, preventing crashes during role updates and other events.

Fixed an issue where the bot would crash due to missing or deleted webhooks when attempting to send messages.

Bug fixes.

Changelog - [2.1.5]

30 August 2024

New Command:

/vote: Users can now earn 10-50 entries by voting if the Chat Rewards system is set up in your server. You can claim entries once every 12 hours.
